Mission

Hacker_quarters [SF] is a hacker guild which values technical proficiency, open access and universal knowledge, sustainable practices, communal quantified self, and above all, mutual excellence.

Manifesto

We hackers do ordain:
  1. Knowledge is to be respected, preserved, protected, valued, admired, crafted, curated, enriched, and made more accessible to the world. Liberators of Knowledge
  2. Sustainable practices be instituted and maintained. Generators of Surplus
  3. Safety and refuge shall be awarded to those who selflessly learn, teach, and apply themselves. But not at the expense of any other rule. Champions of the Academy.
  4. Criticism shall be invited, considered, and deemed the highest level of caring. It shall not met with defensiveness. Embracers of Wisdom
  5. None be granted entry who wish to remain ignorant of the ways of mathematics. Preventors of Ignorance
  6. procrastination not be projected onto others without thought. Practitioners of Productivity
  7. Substances one would not partake in publicly be disallowed. Observers of Moderation
  8. Hack Sabbath will be observed, House attended, and that which is to the benefit of the whole, prevailed. Supporters of the Whole
  9. That thanks and forgiveness be granted to each other when possible. Considerers of Forgiveness
